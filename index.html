Model: Demand = Global IT Spend × AI % × (1 / cost-per-EF-hr) × hours/yr, split into training + inference. Inference demand reduced by efficiency multiplier (distillation, quantization, speculative decoding). Supply = AI-capable GW × compute density (EF/GW) × utilization × constraint factor. Gap = Supply − Demand. Negative = inference crunch.

Key assumptions: Global IT spend $5.5T (2025, Gartner). AI share = CIO-allocated budget (Deloitte: 8-13% 2025, rising). Cost/EF-hr declines with GPU generations (H100 → B200 → Rubin). Efficiency gains compound on inference only, not training.
