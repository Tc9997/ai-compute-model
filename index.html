Methodology: Supply = cumulative installed GPU base × per-chip FP8 ExaFLOPS × bottleneck factors × utilization, capped by datacenter power. Demand = sum of tier token volumes (compounding quarterly growth) × FLOP/token conversion, adjusted for reasoning overhead, frontier model concentration, and training/inference split. Efficiency gains reduce effective demand over time. Gap = Supply − Net Demand.

Sources: Chip shipment estimates from JP Morgan, Morgan Stanley, SemiAnalysis. Power capacity from JLL, CBRE, IEA. Capex from hyperscaler earnings calls (MSFT, GOOG, AMZN, META, ORCL). Efficiency trends from Epoch AI, AI-2027 compute forecast. Token demand estimates are author projections based on usage patterns and announced product roadmaps.
