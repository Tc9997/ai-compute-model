Model: Demand = IT Spend × AI% × (1 - gross margin) × GPU price-perf improvement ÷ base cost-per-EF-hr, lagged N quarters. Inference demand further reduced by algorithmic efficiency multiplier. Supply = GW × density × utilization × constraint, shaped by ramp curve.

Key data: IT spend $5.5T (Gartner 2025). AI budget share 8-13% (Deloitte 2025). GPU price-perf ~2x/gen (H100→B200). Gross margins 45-55% for inference (OpenAI ~70% compute margin, Anthropic ~45%). Deployment lag 12-18 months typical.
